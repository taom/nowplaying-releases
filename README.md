# NowPlayingWidget

**One floating widget for everything you're playing on your Mac.**

NowPlayingWidget stays pinned above every app and Space, and controls whatever's actually
playing — Apple Music, Spotify, Tidal, Deezer, or any browser tab (YouTube, Netflix, and more)
in Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Arc, or Brave. It reads macOS's own system-level Now Playing
layer, the same one Control Center uses, so there's nothing to configure per app.

## Features

- Stays pinned on top of any app or Space
- Click the artwork to jump straight to the app that's actually playing
- Drag to seek, no need to switch apps
- Ambient glow pulled live from the album art
- Three sizes — compact, normal, full — pick what fits your desktop
- Works with 20+ sources: Apple Music, Spotify, Tidal, Deezer, Amazon Music, SoundCloud,
  Pandora, YouTube Music, Audible, Podcasts, Bandcamp, VLC, QuickTime Player, Plex, and any
  browser tab playing audio or video

## Requirements

macOS 13 or later, Apple Silicon.

## What's in this repo

This repo hosts only the release binaries and the [Sparkle](https://sparkle-project.org/)
appcast (`appcast.xml`) that powers in-app auto-updates for NowPlayingWidget. It doesn't
contain the app's source code, which lives in a private repository.
